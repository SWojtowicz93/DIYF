How Adopt

Find Shelter

There is probably a dog shelter near your town

If you have problems finding, please write to us

Contact The Shelter

Many dog shelters have websites where you can contact them

Alternatively, you can visit them (during working hours, of course)

Meet at the Shelter

Meet your pets at the dog shelter

And make a wise decision before adopting(because the dog may not bear to be abandoned again)

Complete the required documents

There should not be a lot of documents

Documents are simple and don't take much time

Usually the adoption costs nothing

But you can always support the shelter with cash or just feed

Every money donated to the shelter helps them keep their pets
